The Correspondents

Lewin wrote to the world. The world wrote back.

This directory indexes every correspondent identified in the Nachlass Louis Lewin at the Staatsbibliothek zu Berlin, drawn from the surviving incoming letters (Lewin's outgoing correspondence does not survive as a collection). Entries vary in completeness as transcription and research continue; every named correspondent is included, even where biographical details are fragmentary. Filters below allow browsing by country, profession, and decade. A searchable name field appears at left.
